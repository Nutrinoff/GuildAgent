Installation


  1. Download and Copy the Google Form:

    1. Make a copy of the Google Form provided here.
    2. The form will be copied to your Google Drive, where you can rename it if needed.

    Create and Set Up a Google Sheet:

      • Return to your Google Form.
      • Click on Responses in the Form editor.
      • Click the green Create Spreadsheet button.
      • Choose to Create a new spreadsheet
      • Get the link to your new spreadsheet and copy the ID from the link. https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/SHEET_ID_HERE/edit

  2. Use Discord Bot Commands:

    1. /setup
    2. Use set up the three necessary channels.
      • Alliance Channel: The channel you want your Alliance guild advertisements
      • Horde Channel: The channel you want your Horde guild advertisements
      • Mod Channel: The channel you want the bot to throw warnings, aka warning you of duplicate entries etc.

    3. /setuptimers
    4. Use the command to set up the age limits for posts. Specify how many days a post must be before it’s reposted and how many days old a post should be before it’s removed.

    5. /setupsheet
    6. Use to configure the Google Sheet. Add your sheet ID here.

If you encounter any issues, please refer to our troubleshooting guide or contact support.
